1. Create and provide permissions of Distributor.

    Permissions of Distributor1
    INCLUDE: IN

    Permissions of Distributor2 < Distributor1
    EXCLUDE: TN-IN

Use (2) to query distributors like:
    Can DISTRIBUTOR2 distribute in ->KLRAI-TN-IN

    Note: Prefix an arrow for locations during querying
